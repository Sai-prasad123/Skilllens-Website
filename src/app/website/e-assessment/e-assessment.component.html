What are E-Assessments ?


E-Assessments are the next level of traditional examinations. Though Online assessments / E-Assessments are considered to be the advancement of traditional examinations, these exams have become the most common. Many competitive examinations like IBPS, SBI, CAT, MAT, IELTS, SSC, RRB, Campus hiring companies, etc have already moved from the traditional mode of conduct of examinations to an internet-based examination system.

At Skill Lens, each exam is created to give a feel of the actual examination. We follow the same test standards and time slots as set by the concerned exam hosting agency. Our exam platform gives you instant results which will help you to understand your areas of deficiency. Getting habituated to online assessments help you reduce nervousness and practice better for the actual exam.
