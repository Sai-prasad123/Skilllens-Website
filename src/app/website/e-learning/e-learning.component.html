How should I choose a Skill?


Choosing a Skill is very simple! However, prior to choosing you should have a Goal. For example, if you have set a goal to get into the banking industry, understand the skill expectations of the banking industry. If you analyze their hiring process, you have to undergo a written test and an interview. The written test in-turn requires Problem Solving skills, English and Verbal skills, Computer skills. Now, figure out the skill areas in which you have better competency, you should work on the other skills which expose your deficiency. Now, your deficient is the Skill you have to choose.
