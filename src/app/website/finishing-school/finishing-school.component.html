Finishing School - SFS


The sole objective of Finishing School is to make students ready for industry. We believe in a “Consistent Training Model”

Abstract


Skills are acquired; but not overnight. The students have to acquire knowledge and the same has to be transformed to skill. Skill Lens Finishing School (SFS) is a place for students who aspire to acquire job skills as per the standards of the industry. At SFS we emphasize on overall personality development. The skill set offered are industry/job specific. At our finishing SCHOOL each student is offered personal attention and the outcomes are pre-defined.

At SFS skill building becomes the learning life style. It’s not just the job skills are learnt, the student’s development is focused on imparting intellectual, mental, emotional, and social abilities. The students are made ready to face the demands, expectations and challenges of everyday life.
