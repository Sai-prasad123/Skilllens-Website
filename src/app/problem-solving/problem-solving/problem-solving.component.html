What is Problem Solving?


Problem-Solving skills helps to estimate the analytical and reasoning skills of a person. These skills are generally categorized as aptitude and reasoning tests by various hiring companies. The outcome of an aptitude test helps us understand the learning potential of an aspirant.

Where is it required?


Problem-solving is a life skill. Problem-solving skills is a combination of both analytical and reasoning skills. Analytical skills are required to analyze, integrate, interpret and make decisions. Logical and reasoning skills depict our creativity, research, risk management, and emotional intelligence.

Teaching Methodology


  • Concepts based solutions
  • Short cut methods
  • Spatial visualization
