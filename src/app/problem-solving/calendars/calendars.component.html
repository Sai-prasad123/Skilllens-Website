The Concept of Calendars

If someone asks you what day it was on 10th May 1575 or what day it would be 12th September 2240, you may call him crazy for asking such simple questions. If you don’t know the rule how to find it, it may look like a Herculean task for you. But, truly speaking, it is not so difficult. In this chapter we will concentrate our discussion on finding its answer i.e., on what day of the week a particular date falls. The clue to the process of finding it lies in calculating the number of odd days, which is quite different from the odd numbers.
