The Concept of Data Sufficiency

Data Sufficiency questions in logical reasoning comprise a few conditions and candidates need to analyse which of the given conditions may help get an answer or solution to the question.

These questions are mostly asked to test the logical and analytic skills of the candidate. Mostly 4-5 data sufficiency questions are asked in the reasoning ability section and they may test your Quant skills as well, and candidates can easily score well in such questions.
