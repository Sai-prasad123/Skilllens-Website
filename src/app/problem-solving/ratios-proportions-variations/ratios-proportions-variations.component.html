The Concept of Ratios, Proportions and Variations

Ratio means comparison- comparing the sizes, the numbers, the quantum of any two or more comparable quantities.

A ratio is a relationship between two numbers by the division process.

The ratio of a to b is written as a: b =
a / b
in the ratio a: b, we can say that as the first term or antecedent and b, the second term or consequent.
