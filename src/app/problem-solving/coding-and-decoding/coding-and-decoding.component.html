The Concept of Coding & Decoding

Coding:

Coding is the process of putting a sequence of characters (letters, numbers, punctuation, and certain symbols) into a specialized format for efficient transmission of a message or storage.

Decoding:

Decoding is the opposite process-the conversion of an encoded format back into the original sequence of characters.

Approach:
  1. Observe alphabets or numbers given in the code keenly.
  2. Find the sequence it follows whether it is ascending or descending.
  3. Detect the rule in which the alphabets/numbers/words follow.
  4. Fill the appropriate letter/number/word in the blank given.
