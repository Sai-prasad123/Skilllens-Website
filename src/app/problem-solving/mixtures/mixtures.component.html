The Concept of Mixtures

when two or more substances mix with each other without participating in a chemical change, the resulting substance is called a Mixture.

  • Proportion of Milk, MP = 
    Milk / Total Volume
  • Proportion of Water, WP = 
    Water / Total Volume
  • % of Milk , M% = 
    Milk / Total Volume
       x 100
  • % of Water , W% = 
    Water / Total Volume
       x 100
  • Ratio of milk and water is M: W
